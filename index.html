Visit Juniper & Stone

We're tucked into a century-old brick building on the corner of Maple and Third, just across from Willowbrook Park. The space is warm and unhurried—exposed beams, natural light, and the kind of atmosphere that invites you to linger over a second cup.

Whether you're here to work, read, or simply watch the neighborhood go by, there's a seat waiting for you.
